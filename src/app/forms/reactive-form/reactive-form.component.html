Reactive Forms

Used for Large forms

Reactive Forms work by creating form control objects in the component class of a template and binding them with HTML form elements

Reactive Forms are also called Model Drive Forms

FormBuilder from ReactiveFormsModule is used to create Reactive Forms. ReactiveFormsModule must be imported in the corresponding module of the form's component and then placed inside the imports:[] field of @NgModule
